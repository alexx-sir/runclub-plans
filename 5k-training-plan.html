The Foundation of This Plan
Everything runs on Zone 2
Almost every run in this plan should feel embarrassingly easy. That's the point. Zone 2 (60–70% max HR) builds your aerobic engine — more mitochondria, better fat burning, improved endurance. The run/walk intervals let you accumulate time on feet without burning out.
✓ The Talk Test
✓You can speak full sentences while running
✓You feel like you could go much faster (but don't)
✓Your breathing is elevated but comfortable
✗If you can't talk — slow down immediately
